How to Play:

  • The computer has challenged you to MatchFour colors in a row!
  • Click to select a color.
  • Click on a box within the first row (your currently selected color will show before you make your guess).
  • Fill in all four boxes with your guesses, then click to confirm your guess.
  • Four circles will display next to your confirmed guess:

    = Correct Slot and Color

    = Correct Color, Wrong Slot

    = Incorrect Color

  • Correctly guess all four colors to win!
  • **BONUS** For a bigger challenge, select "allow for duplicates" under the color palette!
